02 — Brand Story

Two traditions,
one handmade
language

MODO is a handmade lifestyle object brand created by designer Bianca Braga. Working primarily with metal, ceramic, and textile, the brand explores the quiet relationship between material and meaning.

Collaborating with artisans from Brazil and Korea, MODO brings two distinct craft traditions into dialogue—reinterpreting cultural heritage in forms that are both timeless and alive.
